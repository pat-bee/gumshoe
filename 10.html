Course Description

This course is very generally focused on two questions. The first question asks what investments the firm should make. Investments include projects within the firm, acquisitions of other companies, and corporate divestitures, which reverse prior investments. The second question asks how those investments should be financed. Both questions are approached from the perspective of maximizing the value of the firm.
