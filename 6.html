Course Objectives

In recent years, options and other derivatives markets have become increasingly important in the world of finance and investments. Thus, it is essential for all finance professionals to understand how these markets work, how these derivatives can be used, and what determines the prices of derivatives. This course addresses these issues and also examines trading strategies involving options.
