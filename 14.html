Course Objectives

The course begins with a very brief review to basic statistical concepts. We will further develop the basic ideas that you have learnt and we will analyze complex data schemes. Practitioners are often confronted with different type of data. They could be ratio, categorical or ordinal scales and this will require different approaches and decisions. Course Description

Small Data is an integral part of Business Analytics and it refers to two important concepts in practice: When solving a consulting problem, it is very common to have a small sized data set (say less than 30) in order to solve practical business situations. This data set need not be normal or could be quantitative or qualitative.
