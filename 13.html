Course Description

Financial statements are widely used among finance professionals to assess the financial health of a company and to assist with economic decision-making. However, used improperly, these statements may lead to suboptimal forecasting, valuation, and other investment and credit-related decisions. Financial Statement Analysis is designed to better prepare students to interpret, analyze, and adjust financial statements in making such assessments regarding firms’ financial well-being.
